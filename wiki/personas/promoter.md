# Persona: Event Promoter

> Status: **Stub.** First-pass; Doug to tighten with real interviews.

## Snapshot
Independent promoter or small collective running club nights, festivals, or showcases in underground electronic music. Books 5–20 artists per event.

## Goals
- Find rising artists worth booking before they get expensive
- Promote each line-up across multiple platforms in the weeks before the event
- Build a recognisable promoter brand voice that travels across artist announcements

## Pain points
- Booking research = manually checking SoundCloud / Resident Advisor / IG; slow
- Per-artist line-up announcements are repetitive to write but matter for ticket sales
- Cross-platform scheduling is fiddly; missed posts = missed ticket window

## How Sound Cave helps
- **Foraging** — targeted searches for the genre / vibe of an upcoming event
- **Firepit Forge** — bulk-generate per-artist line-up announcement copy in a consistent promoter voice
- **Trail Map** — schedule the announcement campaign across platforms in one pass

## Anti-fit
Mainstream / pop / corporate event promoters. Promoters who outsource all marketing to an agency.
