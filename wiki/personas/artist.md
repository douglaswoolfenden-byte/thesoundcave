# Persona: Artist

> Status: **Stub.** First-pass; Doug to tighten with real interviews.

## Snapshot
A solo or duo act in underground/electronic music (the genre Sound Cave currently scouts). Self-managed or with a small team. Releases tracks regularly, plays gigs, runs their own social presence.

## Goals
- Stay visible between releases without spending all day on social
- Promote new tracks across multiple platforms in a coherent voice
- Spot which peers are rising (collab targets, scene awareness)

## Pain points
- Content is the second job; they didn't sign up to be a marketer
- Generic AI tools (ChatGPT) don't know underground music culture and produce embarrassing copy
- Manually cross-posting to IG / TikTok / X / SoundCloud is tedious

## How Sound Cave helps
- **Foraging + Clan** — track scene peers without manual research
- **Firepit Forge** — generate on-brand posts about own + peer releases
- **Trail Map** — schedule a week's worth of content in one sitting

## Anti-fit
Major-label artists with full PR teams. Pop / mainstream artists outside the scout's genre coverage.
