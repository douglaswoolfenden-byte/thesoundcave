What is The Sound Cave?

The Sound Cave is a music discovery engine built for the underground. It automatically scouts SoundCloud for unsigned and emerging artists across 16 dance music genres, tracks their growth over time, and gives you the tools to curate, manage and promote the ones worth watching. Think of it as your personal A&R assistant for the European underground scene.

Terminology

🏹
Foraging

Live artist search. Hunt for new talent by genre, follower count and more. Schedule automated searches to run while you sleep.

🦴
Clan

Your roster of tracked artists. View profiles, link platforms, star favourites and monitor their growth from one place.

🔥
The Cave

Your dashboard. See aggregate stats, genre breakdowns, weekly charts and new track drops from your Clan at a glance.

👣
Footprints

Analytics deep dive. Per-artist growth charts, week-over-week metrics and insights on who's rising and who's fading.

🪨
Firepit

AI content production. Generate social posts, press releases, event copy and more — powered by Claude, tuned for underground music culture.

✂️
Cut

Remove an artist from active tracking. Cut artists stop appearing in your dashboard and analytics but their data is preserved.

How It Works

1
Search

Use Foraging to search SoundCloud by genre, follower range and keywords. Get results instantly or schedule recurring hunts.

2
Curate

Add promising artists to your Clan. Link their other platforms, star your favourites, add notes.

3
Track

Daily snapshots automatically collect follower, play and like data. Watch growth rates in The Cave and Footprints.

4
Promote

Use the Firepit to generate social media posts, press releases and event copy for your artists. Export reports to share.

Why does data take time to appear?

Charts and growth metrics require at least two data points to draw a comparison. The Sound Cave collects daily snapshots via an automated tracker, so you'll start seeing meaningful trends within a few days of adding artists to your Clan. Weekly scout reports add additional depth. The more time passes, the richer and more accurate your analytics become.
