# Persona: Label

> Status: **Stub.** First-pass; Doug to tighten with real interviews.

## Snapshot
Independent / boutique electronic music label. 5–50 artists on the roster. Small team (1–5 people) doing A&R, marketing, release coordination.

## Goals
- Discover unsigned artists worth signing or licensing
- Produce release-week content for every artist on the roster, consistently
- Track artist growth over time to inform A&R and marketing spend

## Pain points
- A&R = manually trawling SoundCloud / Bandcamp; doesn't scale
- Content output drops off for non-priority releases because the marketing team is overloaded
- No single view of how the roster is performing week-on-week

## How Sound Cave helps
- **Scout + Foraging** — automated A&R pipeline
- **Clan + Footprints** — roster dashboard with real growth data
- **Firepit Forge + Trail Map** — produce + schedule release content for every artist, not just the top three

## Anti-fit
Major labels (own tooling, own data teams). Non-electronic labels (scout's genre coverage doesn't fit yet).
